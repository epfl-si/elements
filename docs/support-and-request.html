Support & request



The EPFL’s styleguide team is committed to maintaining a living and quality documentation of its design system. We are welcoming any feedback given on bugs, designs issues or new ideas. You can find all the information about contribution right here.
Elements is maintain by Mediacom (the communication department of EPFL). All requests are send there, then dispatched to the appropriate people.

If you have a pressing bug, it is best to make a Pull Request (PR) for the issue. Our team will try to address your bug as soon as possible. Issues that are out of scope will be closed until it becomes a higher priority. Thank you for your understanding.

The styleguide is Web first. We provide a CMS-agnostic or technology-agnostic design documentation. That's why you will only find components that match web standards. That doesn't mean you can't document your own project. We'll be happy to reference your work from other platforms here! Reach out to us.
